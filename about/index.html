About Us

A macOS product studio building focused, local-first tools for real work.

ProductivitySurge creates focused desktop applications for the work people already do — writing and documentation, document organization, storage hygiene, and local media workflows.

We do not build one oversized suite. We ship independent tools you can adopt one at a time, each designed around a clear purpose and a local-first philosophy.

Our story

Digital work is full of small, expensive frustrations: files with meaningless names, folders that never get cleaned, Markdown workflows that fragment across tools, and media libraries that are hard to browse or keep visually consistent.

ProductivitySurge exists to turn those frustrations into focused, practical Mac apps. Each product owns one job. Prefer preview before irreversible changes, keep processing local whenever practical, and make support easy to find.

What we believe

One product, one job

Each app should solve its primary problem exceptionally well before expanding scope.

Local-first

Core work stays on your Mac when a product does not genuinely need the cloud.

User control

You should understand what will happen before irreversible actions run.

Grow the suite, not the bloat

New apps expand ProductivitySurge without turning existing tools into megasuites.

Real workflows

Shipping decisions come from operator needs, testing, and feedback — not feature theater.

Clarity & trust

Transparent product information, dependable behavior, and responsive support.

Mission

Our mission is to create focused, privacy-conscious software that simplifies digital work, improves organization, and gives users greater control over the tools and information they rely on every day.

Short form: build practical software that reduces friction and helps people work smarter.

Vision

Build a trusted and growing ecosystem of productivity applications that solve real problems through clarity, thoughtful automation, user control, and purposeful design.
